About the Villain Origin Test

How the Villain Origin Test Works

The Villain Origin Test presents 16 rapid-fire binary choices. No overthinking allowed, just pure instinct. Tap left or right for each dilemma, and your pattern of choices reveals which villain archetype matches your personality.

Understanding Villain Archetypes

Each villain archetype represents a different psychological response to adversity. From the calculating Mastermind to the chaotic Anarchist, these archetypes explore the darker side of human motivation in a fun, fictional context.
